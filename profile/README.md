## Jelly Recipe

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Fresh ripe strawberries | `3.5 lbs` | `1.6 kg` |
| Strained strawberry juice | `3.5 cups` | `830 ml` |
| Powdered fruit pectin | `1.75 oz` | `49 g` |
| Bottled lemon juice | `2 tbsp` | `30 ml` |
| Granulated sugar | `4.5 cups` | `900 g` |

### Quick Steps

1. **Extract Juice**  
   Crush strawberries and simmer with **½ cup (120 ml)** water for 10 minutes. Strain through cheesecloth into a bowl *without squeezing* until you have **3.5 cups (830 ml)** of clear juice.

2. **Boil Pectin**  
   Combine juice, lemon juice, and pectin in a pot. Bring to a full rolling boil over high heat.

3. **Add Sugar & Boil**  
   Stir in sugar, return to a full rolling boil, and boil hard for **exactly 1 minute**, stirring constantly.

4. **Jar & Set**  
   Remove from heat, skim off foam, ladle into clean jars, and let set undisturbed for 12 to 24 hours.
